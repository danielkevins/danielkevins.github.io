Bike Sales Dashboard

Summary

This analysis aims to analyze customer and bicycle purchase data using a comprehensive data-driven approach using Microsoft Excel. I started by exploring and cleaning the original dataset to ensure consistency and quality of the data. Next, I developed an in-depth analysis by utilizing pivot tables and various visualization techniques. I categorized demographic data, analyzed relationships between attributes, and identified potential customer profiles based on factors such as income, age, and travel distance. These findings were then summarized in the form of interactive visualizations on a dashboard, making the data more understandable to a wider audience.
